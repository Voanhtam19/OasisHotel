CHOICES

Standard room 

This is the most basic type of hotel room, typically with one queen- or king-sized bed.

Twin room

This room has two twin beds, which are smaller than double beds. Twin rooms are ideal for single travelers or friends traveling together.

King room

This room has one king-sized bed, the largest standard bed size.

Suite

A suite is a larger room or series of rooms that includes a sleeping area, a living area, and sometimes a kitchenette or wet bar. Suites are often more luxurious than standard hotel rooms and may offer additional amenities such as a Jacuzzi or fireplace.

Executive suite

This type of suite is designed for business travelers and may offer amenities such as a desk, ergonomic chair, and high-speed internet access.

Presidential suite

The most luxurious type of suite, a presidential suite is typically very large and has multiple bedrooms, bathrooms, and living areas. It may also have amenities such as a butler or private balcony.
